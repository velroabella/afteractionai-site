How It Works

From conversation to action plan — in one session

No appointments. No forms. No waiting. Tell AfterAction AI your situation — it does the analysis and produces a structured report you can act on immediately.

Step 01

Share Your Situation

Upload documents — DD-214, denial letters, medical records, resume — or just start talking. The AI meets you where you are and asks the right questions.

Step 02

The AI Analyzes

AfterAction AI cross-references your input against thousands of benefits, programs, and resources in real time — identifying gaps, opportunities, and next steps specific to you.

Step 03

Receive Your Report

A structured written report with immediate actions, short-term goals, and long-range strategy — organized by priority, ready to execute.
