Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Yes. Completely free. No subscription, no paywall, no hidden fees. Veterans earned benefits through service — we believe the tools to claim them should be free. We're supported by grants and mission-aligned partnerships, not by charging veterans.

We don't sell your data, store it permanently, or share it without your consent. Documents you upload are processed to generate your benefits plan, then securely deleted. We use anonymous analytics to improve the service. Crisis information is routed to the Veterans Crisis Line but never stored in our system. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

No. AfterAction AI is an independent nonprofit. We're not affiliated with the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs or the Department of Defense. However, we work with the VA system and complement (not replace) official VA resources. Always verify benefits information directly with VA.gov or your local VA office.

AfterAction AI was founded by Mike Jackson, a retired Senior Master Sergeant in the U.S. Air Force with 25 years of service. After years working with transitioning servicemembers, he saw how many veterans missed out on benefits they'd earned. He built this tool to change that. Learn more about us.

Yes. Your documents are encrypted in transit and at rest, processed securely to extract relevant information, and then deleted from our servers. We don't retain a copy of your DD-214 or other military documents. That said, we recommend redacting sensitive personal information (SSN, account numbers) before uploading any document to any service.

Our AI matches your service history and profile to thousands of known benefits and programs. But we're not perfect — and benefits rules change. Always verify recommendations with official sources (VA.gov, state veteran agencies, VSOs). Think of us as a starting point, not a final word. If you believe we've missed a benefit you qualify for, tell us.

Absolutely. We're actively building partnerships with VSOs, nonprofits, federal agencies, and state veteran programs. If your organization helps veterans and you'd like to explore collaboration, get in touch. We can discuss data integration, co-marketing, or joint initiatives.

We're actively recruiting board members with expertise in nonprofit operations, fundraising, technology, or strong ties to the veteran community. If you're interested in joining our leadership, contact us with a brief description of your background and why you want to serve. See current board info.

We're grateful for your support.
