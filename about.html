About AfterAction AI

Built by a veteran, for veterans — our mission is to connect every service member to every benefit they've earned.

Our Mission

AfterAction AI connects every veteran to every benefit, resource, and organization they've earned — free, in one conversation, with a personalized action plan delivered instantly.

Why This Exists

200,000+
Veterans Separate Annually
Every year, over 200,000 service members transition to civilian life — many unaware of the benefits they've earned.
$72B+
In Unclaimed Benefits
Billions in federal, state, and nonprofit benefits go unused annually because veterans don't know they exist.
100+
Fragmented Programs
VA benefits, state programs, nonprofit resources, and employment support are scattered across government and private organizations.

Our Founders

Mike Jackson

Co-Founder

Senior Master Sergeant (Ret.) Mike S. Jackson is a 25-year U.S. Air Force veteran and senior aviation leader with deep expertise in aviation operations, training systems, and enterprise-level program management. Throughout his career, he led high-impact initiatives that identified inefficiencies, optimized processes, and improved operational readiness across large, complex organizations. As Co-Founder of AfterAction AI, he applies that same problem-solving mindset to streamline systems and improve the lives of veterans through intelligent, mission-driven automation.
